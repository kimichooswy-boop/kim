Alta Shopping Complex
Reflective of class and sophistication, Alta offers a high-end retail experience, celebrating the latest innovative fashion, from prestigious designer labels. Situated in Central Flacq, the shopping complex consists of a modern structure featuring 6 separate levels – 2 levels of underground parking, 3 levels of retail outlets, bars and restaurants, and a rooftop level with additional parking.
The external area features large grassed areas with ample seating and a large lake that is home to an array of local flora and fauna. This luxury and exclusive establishment is represented by the lily flower, symbol of nobility, and visualised through a logo and pattern that can be found on packagings, bags and banners, reinforcing the brand identity.
Olive Bird Cafe

Olive Bird is a cafe, located at Ebene Commercial Centre, a prime location targeting office workers in need of a coffee boost. The journey of the owner Sam Garny, as a coffee artists that has travelled all over the world to learn everything that has to be known about coffee, is reflected through the café’s menu.

The name of the cafe and its logo were inspired by the Olive-backed Sunbird, which helps in the pollination of coffee plants. It stands out as it gives customers the feeling of travelling through the exotic-tasting coffees and meals, taking them away from the mundane and repetitive office worklife.

Cutesy Panda
Stemming from her undying love for pandas, Mrs Neuk Moy Ah-Poa desired a gift shop that revolved around her favourite animal. Based in Plaine Magnien, the shop proposes a variety of items that will satisfy customers of all ages. The brand identity revolves around pandas and bamboos. The aesthetic is quite feminine and playful, creating a sense of warmth and approachability.
This project involved working with a real client, resulting in learning how to create and adapt to what the client wants, how to communicate with them and integrate their feedback. It was definitely a different experience, as the most important feedback came from the client, who does not have any background or knowledge in design, in contrast to my tutors and peers who were the ones giving me feedbacks, in my previous projects.
Huggies Ultra Dry Nappies
The advertising campaign consists of 3 A4 print ads, for the Ultra Dry Nappies of the brand Huggies. As a product for babies, the concept is designed to reflect innocence, which is reinforced by the use of soft pastel colours and cuteness bias, created through the visual of babies and animals that would immediately speak to the soft spot of the target audience - the parents. The ads each showcase the baby in a different weather and position, as a way to demonstrate the adaptability of the diaper in different temperatures and movements.
High Heels Catalogue
The High Heel Fashion Guide is an informative catalogue dedicated to high heels. From fashion history to fashion tips, the catalogue is a source of enjoyment for fashion lovers and enthusiasts, wanting to learn in a fun and colourful way.
The catalogue consists of 32 pages with a matte finish, including the front and back covers.There are 14 pairs of shoes of different category, which are each featured on a dedicated double page spread with information. The catalogue is in A4 format (210 x 297 mm) and portrait orientation, with the cover having a thick 250 GSM for durability, while inner papers have a 150 GSM.
Movie Festival Posters
The Curtin Student Guild is organising a film festival. The lineup is made up of 8 movies, all related to school, giving the festival the name of School Life. The schedule poster includes all the information regarding the movie lineup, the venue, time and sponsors. It also uses basic shapes to illustrate a connection between school and movies through a whiteboard and film tapes.
Two A3 movie posters, of the most popular movies are made to advertise the event. Clueless and High School Musical, being the most requested movies from Curtin University students, were chosen to further advertise the event. The visual is inspired by the style of colouring books for a fun and nostalgic effect, with the main characters depicted during iconic scenes of the movies.
